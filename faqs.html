Frequently Asked Questions

What is EV Revival?

A web-based support and guidance site for Vestel EV charger owners.

Does EV Revival support all chargers?

No. Vestel chargers only.

Does EV Revival guarantee my charger schedule will work?

No. Scheduling and commands depend on charger, firmware, connectivity, and third-party systems.

Why is my charger showing offline?

Most commonly due to Wi-Fi/router issues, signal strength, backend settings, or charger firmware behavior.

How does the charger connect?

Via your home network/Wi-Fi settings configured in the charger interface.

What if my Wi-Fi is weak?

Try moving router/extender, or use a powerline adapter. Distance, walls, and garage structure matter.

Why are commands not going through?

The charger may be offline, backend details may be wrong, or network routing/DNS may be blocking communication.

Can I use this like an app on my phone?

Yes. Add it to your home screen from your browser.

Do I need a backend?

For OCPP-driven remote features, usually yes.

What happens if my charger disconnects from Wi-Fi?

Online features and commands can fail until it reconnects.

Can EV Revival control my home network?

No.

What should I try if my charger keeps disconnecting?

Restart router/charger, improve signal, test extender/powerline, and verify saved Wi-Fi details.

Is mesh Wi-Fi recommended?

It can work, but in my experience a standard extender or powerline adapter often works better for these chargers.

Who should I contact if the charger still faults?

Contact an EV specialist, installer, or manufacturer support.

What does 30 days free mean?

You can use the service for 30 days before paid pricing starts.

What happens after the free trial?

Current price is £3/month, subject to future updates.

How do Login and Billing work?

For now, they are placeholders for a future release and are not yet live.
