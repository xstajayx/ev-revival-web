Cookie Policy

Last updated: 6 April 2026

EV Revival explains cookie use and consent choices on first visit.

Essential cookies: needed for sign-in, security, and core site operation.

Analytics cookies: help improve performance and content. These are optional where consent is required.

Preference cookies: remember settings such as UI preferences.

How to control cookies: customers can manage optional cookies via a cookie banner or settings link, and can also use browser controls.

Third-party cookies: if used, EV Revival identifies categories and explains their purpose.
