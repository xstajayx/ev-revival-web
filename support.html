Support

If you need help with your EV Revival account, Subject Access Requests (SAR), billing history, or data deletion, please use the contact details provided on this page.

Support channels

  • Email support: support@jworldcreations.com
  • For billing questions, include your account email and payment date.
  • For SAR requests, include the account email and request "Subject Access Request".
  • Response times may vary, and support is provided on a reasonable-efforts basis.

Subject Access Request (SAR)

  • You can request your personal data, including account profile, RFID tags, and session history for the most recent 60 days.
  • You can download usage history in-app from Account Settings using the "Download Data" option.
  • Session history is retained for up to 60 days and then deleted automatically.
  • For billing receipts and invoices, use the Stripe customer portal (or billing area inside the app where available).

See Privacy Policy for full details on retention and data access rights.

Data deletion and retained records

  • When your account is deleted, personal data is erased from EV Revival systems.
  • Charger serial numbers may be retained for trial verification, fraud-prevention, and service protection.
  • RFID tags are available while your account is active.
