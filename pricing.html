Pricing

Simple pricing for UK Vestel charger owners, with clear trial and subscription terms.

Premium plan

£3.99/month

Recurring monthly billing

  • 14-day free trial for new customers.
  • Trial starts when you first activate premium access in the app.
  • After trial expiry, payment is required to continue premium access.
  • If payment fails, premium features may be restricted until billing is active again.
Start in the App Read FAQs

What is included

  • Vestel-focused setup and connectivity guidance.
  • Troubleshooting support for common charger states and simple diagnostic visibility where supported.
  • OCPP connection help and practical support resources for supported chargers.
  • Access to current help content and product improvements during active billing.

Cancellation and after cancellation

  • You can cancel recurring billing from your EV Revival account area when available.
  • After cancellation, access normally continues until the end of your paid period.
  • When billing becomes inactive, premium features may be paused while your core account data/settings may be retained for a limited period to support reactivation and support requests.
  • Retention periods and full details are explained in Privacy Policy and Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Review the Trial & Subscription Terms before subscribing. Billing information on this page matches the live service terms.
