About EV Revival

I built EV Revival from real hands-on experience with Vestel EV chargers and the support challenges owners can face.

I kept seeing the same pattern: charger apps and support journeys were often unclear, too technical, or hard to follow when something went wrong.

So I created EV Revival to explain things in plain English, especially around connectivity, setup, and common charger issues.

This site does not pretend to replace manufacturer support, installer advice, or specialist diagnostics. My aim is to make everyday steps clearer, reduce frustration, and help owners make better decisions faster.

Everything here is practical, grounded, and focused on Vestel chargers only.
