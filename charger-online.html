Get Your Charger Online

Check your charger model first. HS chargers have “HS” in the model number shown on the side label.

A) Vestel HS chargers

Login IP:

192.168.35.1

Username: admin

Password: printed in your supplied booklet (charger-specific). Example only: F4De*5/gf. Do not assume the example password will work on your charger.

  1. Identify HS model from side label.
  2. Connect your phone/laptop to charger/local Wi-Fi if required.
  3. Open your browser and go to 192.168.35.1.
  4. Log in with your own booklet password.
  5. Open network/Wi-Fi settings.
  6. Enter home Wi-Fi details and save.
  7. Reboot charger and retry.
  8. If page does not load, disconnect mobile data/VPN and retry.
  9. If phone switches networks, reconnect to the charger network.
  10. If charger will not reconnect, restart router and charger, then retry.

B) Vestel non-HS chargers

Login IP:

192.168.1.1

E.ON non-HS login: username admin / password eon01.

  1. Check label confirms non-HS model.
  2. Connect to charger/local Wi-Fi if needed.
  3. Open browser and go to 192.168.1.1.
  4. Log in with matching credentials.
  5. Find Wi-Fi/network settings.
  6. Connect charger to home Wi-Fi and save.
  7. Restart charger and verify online status.
  8. If no page load, check device is on same local network.
  9. If your phone keeps moving to another network, disable auto-join temporarily.
  10. If reconnect fails repeatedly, move router/extender closer or escalate support.

Connectivity Tips

  • The charger relies on your own home Wi-Fi/network.
  • If the charger disconnects, commands/signals may fail.
  • This is outside the control of EV Revival.
  • If signal is weak, try a Wi-Fi extender or powerline adapter.
  • In my experience, a standard extender or powerline adapter often works better than a mesh setup for these chargers.
  • Walls, distance, garages, and weak-signal areas can all affect reliability.
