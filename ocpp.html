Connect to OCPP

OCPP is a standard way for chargers to talk to a backend platform. You may need this so your charger can report status and receive commands.

  1. Log in to your charger settings.
  2. Find backend/OCPP settings in the menu.
  3. Enter the OCPP URL from your backend provider.
  4. Save or apply changes.
  5. Reboot/restart the charger if needed.
  6. Check charger online status from your platform.

Exact menus may vary by charger firmware.

A charger may still fail to connect if the home network, Wi-Fi, firmware, DNS, routing, or backend details are incorrect.
