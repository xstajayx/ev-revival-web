Connect Your Vestel Charger to EV Revival (OCPP)

Once your charger is on Wi-Fi, this takes only a few minutes. Follow these steps in order and you should be connected successfully.

Before you start

  • Have your charger CBID ready from the side label.
  • Make sure the charger has already been set up on home Wi-Fi.
  • If needed, use the Get Online page first, then return here.

Step 1: Log in using the same charger page steps as Get Online

  1. Power-cycle the charger at the supply and wait until blue/green flashing stops.
  2. Connect your device to the charger hotspot (network starts with 700) using your CBID password.
  3. If your device says Connected without internet, stay connected.
  4. Open the correct charger login page and sign in (HS: 192.168.35.1, non-HS: 192.168.1.1).
  5. If the page does not open, reconnect to the charger hotspot and try again.

Step 2: Go to the OCPP tab

  1. In the charger web UI, open the menu.
  2. Select OCPP.

Step 3: Update Central System Address

  1. Find Central System Address.
  2. Enter: wss://ocpp.jworldcreations.com/ws/CBID
  3. Replace CBID with your own charger ID.

Step 4: Save correctly

After changing Central System Address, press Save on the OCPP page only.

Do not press Save again on the Main Page, because this may revert the address to default.

After saving

  • Give the charger a few minutes to reconnect.
  • If connection fails, check CBID spelling in the address and save again on the OCPP page only.
  • If needed, repeat from Step 1 and make sure your device stayed connected to the charger hotspot during login.

Example

Example CBID: EU_EDR_CB12001234

Example full Central System Address:

wss://ocpp.jworldcreations.com/ws/EU_EDR_CB12001234
